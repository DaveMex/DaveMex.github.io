About me

Mexican Graphic Designer, lived and studied between 2004 and 2009 in London UK. Currently in Mexico City, worked at a consultancy/marketing agency, previously at Milenio DatLab. Design professor at Universidad Anáhuac del Norte. Has a Masters degree from Central Saint Martins College of Art and Design, University of the Arts London and a BA from the Universidad Iberoamericana. Experienced in advertising Art Direction, editorial design, illustration, UX/UI design and primarily in Information Design.
